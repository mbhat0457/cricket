Cricket is a sport which involves a bat and a ball

The Definition of Cricket is: an open-air game played on a large grass field with ball, bats, and two wickets, between teams of eleven players, the object of the game being to score more runs than the opposition.

One of the Greatest Games Ever Played

This is how cricket is played according to the Chicago Tribune

Cricket is played with two teams of 11 players each. Each team takes turns batting and bowling. The batter is a batsman and the person bowling is the bowler. The bowler tries to knock down what is known as a wicket to strike out the batsman and prevent him from scoring.
